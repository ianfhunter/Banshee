Weapon Damage:
- Piercing
- Bludgeoning
- Slashing

Elemental Types
- Fire
- Water
- Air
- Wind

Para-Elemental Types
- Poison
- Acid
- Ice
- Sonic
- Psychic
- Necrotic
- Radiant

The following are not damage types, but when attacking certain creatures confer certain bonuses:
- Silver
- Cold Iron


- Poison damage inflicts damage over time.
